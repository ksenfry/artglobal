Initiatives for humanity

OUR STORY

I think art, especially ballet, is awesome. It's not just something we do to express ourselves, but also something we do to connect with others. It can foster a more peaceful and harmonious world, and create positive change in the global community.

That's why I started ART GLOBAL INITIATIVES, a non-profit that uses art and art education to do cool stuff around the world that promotes global unity, peace, equalities, and helps people with various problems. I think that art can connect people who come from different places, traditions, and beliefs, and make them feel more united and peaceful.




core

BELIEVES

Power of Art

Art is a way to connect with others and contribute to the society. Art can inspire us, challenge us, and transform us.

Art and Unity

We believe that art can bridge the gaps between people from different backgrounds, cultures, and religions.

Art and Education

We believe that art can enrich the lives of children and youth, and foster their learning and development.

 

Art and Justice

We believe that art can empower the marginalized and voiceless, and advocate for their rights and dignity.

Art and Health

We believe that art can heal the wounds of people who are affected by diseases, disabilities, or trauma, and give them hope and resilience.

Art and

Art can make us see the world in a new light, and create a positive impact in the world


 

Founder's Personal Journey.

Dear Friends and Supporters, I am thrilled to share the story of our incredible journey, a tale of passion, creativity, and a relentless commitment to making a positive impact on our world.
The seeds of our organization were planted in the soil of a shared belief—that art has the power to transform not just canvas, but entire communities. Fueled by this conviction, we embarked on a mission to weave the threads of creativity into the fabric of global problem-solving.
Our story is one of resilience and adaptability, navigating the ever-changing landscapes of social and environmental issues with the unwavering belief that art is not just a medium of expression, but a force for positive change. Witnessing the transformative impact of our endeavors has been nothing short of magical.
Yet, our story is far from complete. As we continue to evolve, so too does the canvas upon which we paint our dreams of a better world. Together, we look ahead to a future where our organization's story becomes a beacon of hope, inspiring others to pick up the brush - or put on ballet shoes - and join us in the pursuit of a more harmonious and sustainable world.
Thank you for being a part of our story. Together, let us create a legacy of creativity, compassion, and change.
With gratitude,
Emily Fry
