OUR AREAS

Our initiatives are to solve issues in one of the following areas

Peace and unity

Art can transcend the boundaries of language, culture, and geography, and connect people through shared emotions and experiences. Art can also inspire people to imagine a better world, and to work together to make it a reality.

Cultural diversity and intercultural dialogue

Art can foster mutual understanding and respect among people from different backgrounds, cultures, and identities. Art can also celebrate the richness and diversity of human expression, and promote cross-cultural collaboration and exchange.

Education and lifelong learning

Art can enhance the quality and accessibility of education for all, especially for children and youth who are deprived of learning opportunities. Art can also stimulate creativity and innovation, and develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills that are essential for the 21st century.

Human rights and social justice

Art can expose and challenge the systemic injustices and inequalities that affect marginalized and oppressed groups in society. Art can also amplify the voices and stories of those who are often silenced or ignored, and advocate for their rights and dignity.

Health and well-being

Art can improve the physical and mental health and well-being of individuals and communities, especially for those who are suffering from illness, trauma, or stress. Art can also provide a source of joy and happiness, and enhance the sense of belonging and purpose in life.

Environmental sustainability

Art can raise awareness and mobilize action on the urgent issues of environmental degradation. Art can also envision and propose alternative ways of living in harmony with nature.
