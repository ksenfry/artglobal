Ballet for Hope

Initiative

Details

Date:
Ongoing
Whom are we helping:
Children effected by world conflict
Essense:
An initiative that uses ballet to provide emotional support and psychological relief to children who live in conflict zones or relocated due to a conflict.

Description

We believe that ballet can help children cope with the stress and trauma of war, and restore their sense of normalcy, hope and dignity. We organize ballet workshops and lessons that allow children to learn the basics of ballet, and to express their feelings, fears, and dreams through dance. We collaborate with local ballet teachers, dancers, and therapists to facilitate these workshops and provide a safe and supportive environment for the children. We also arrange ballet performances that showcase the children's talents and stories to the public and the media, to raise awareness and empathy for their situation.
